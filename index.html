Our Services

Primary Care

Comprehensive health assessments, illness treatment, and preventive care for all students. Our primary care physicians are trained in adolescent medicine and provide personalized care plans tailored to each student's needs.

Mental Health Support

Confidential counseling and mental wellness resources for students and staff. Our licensed therapists provide individual and group therapy sessions, stress management workshops, and crisis intervention services.

First Aid & Emergency Care

Immediate treatment for injuries and emergencies with follow-up care plans. Our clinic is equipped to handle minor injuries, allergic reactions, and emergency situations with rapid response protocols.

Immunizations

Vaccinations and health screenings to meet school requirements and ensure student safety. We provide all required school vaccinations as well as flu shots and travel vaccinations for school-sponsored trips.
